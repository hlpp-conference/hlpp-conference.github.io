HLPP Conference Series

The International Symposium on High-Level Parallel Programming and Applications (HLPP) serves as a forum for researchers and engineers in parallel computing, offering an excellent opportunity for scientists to present their latest ideas and findings in this challenging and ever-changing research field.

HLPP focuses on high-level parallel programming of multi-/many-core platforms, accelerator architectures, heterogeneous compute clusters, and highly parallel or distributed infrastructures.
Of particular Interest are innovative approaches in parallel programming models and applications, high-level parallel languages, compilers, runtime systems, and programming tools for all classes of parallel machines.
